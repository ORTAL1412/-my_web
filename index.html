Come and experience the difference for yourself. Gel polish is a long-lasting variety of nail polish made up of a type of methacrylate polymer.
It is painted on the nail similar to traditional nail polish, but does not dry.
Instead it is cured under an ultraviolet lamp or ultraviolet LED. While regular nail polish formulas
typically last two to seven days without chipping, gel polish can last as long as two weeks with proper
application and home care. Gel polish can be more difficult to remove than regular nail polish.
It is usually removed by soaking the nails in pure acetone
(the solvent used in most nail polish removers) for five to fifteen minutes, depending on the formula.
